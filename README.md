FMCG Field Sales & Target Tracking Dashboard

An interactive, data-driven FMCG Field Sales Dashboard designed as a daily co-pilot for sales representatives and commercial teams. This tool transforms complex retail data into immediate commercial insights, optimizing daily store visits, tracking sales targets, and identifying distribution gaps at the Point of Sale (POS).

📊 Dashboard Breakdown & Field Application

1. Sales Performance & Commercial Overview

This tab serves as the sales representative's daily starting point. It provides an immediate health check of the territory's sales compared to set targets and previous years.

Key Field Applications:

Target vs. Actuals Tracking: Instantly see where you stand regarding the current month's targets to prioritize commercial push.

Category Share Evolution: Monitor category performance (e.g., Cat1 maintaining a dominant 62-64% share) to ensure shelf space matches sales momentum.

Customer Prioritization: Spot top-tier accounts (e.g., Customer 1, Customer 27) showing significant target gaps and plan corrective actions before visiting.

2. SKU Performance & Numeric Distribution Analysis

Focused entirely on in-store execution and portfolio expansion. It helps the representative identify exactly which products are winning or losing ground in retail points.

Key Field Applications:

Numeric Distribution Gap Identification: Track the Distr% (Numeric Distribution) to see which high-volume SKUs are missing from shelves (e.g., targeting stores where a key product's distribution dropped).

Distribution Point Change (Δ 2025 vs 2026): The visual green/red bar chart highlights immediate distribution wins and losses per product. This serves as a perfect pitch guide for the representative's next retail visit to reclaim lost shelf placements.

Volume Contribution: Evaluate if category-level sales drops are driven by specific underperforming SKUs.

3. Customer Status & Field Route Optimizer

Designed to assist in territory management, route planning, and customer relationship recovery. It provides exact geographic and status details for every retail outlet.

Key Field Applications:

Churn Prevention & Account Recovery: Representatives can filter customers by "Not this year" or "Never" to immediately identify churned or dormant accounts and schedule reactivation visits.

Efficient Route Planning: Leverage exact address data (Region, City, Street) alongside commercial performance to plan geographical routes, saving transit time and maximizing daily store visit impact.

Client Health Audit: Quickly check the status of active clients to review who is growing (26 vs 25 % positive delta) and who requires immediate commercial support.

🛠️ Technical Stack & Features

Power BI Desktop: Core dashboard design and visualization layout.

DAX (Data Analysis Expressions): Used for advanced time-intelligence calculations (YoY Growth, Target Achievement rates) and dynamic status classification (Active, Never, Not this year).
